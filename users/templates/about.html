Challenge that a Stock Newbie faces

The investors who put in money systematically, in the right shares and held on to their investments patiently have been seen generating outstanding returns. However, a typical buyer's decision is usually heavily influenced by the actions of his acquaintances, neighbours or relatives, if everybody around is investing in a particular stock, the tendency for potential investors is to do the same.

Problem with this approach

But this strategy is bound to backfire in the long run. Since not everyone has a sound knowledge of how the stock market works and what various factors need to be considered before making an investment.
